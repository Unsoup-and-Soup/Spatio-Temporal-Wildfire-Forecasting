Spatio-Temporal Wildfire Forecasting

Hello User!

Please select a point on the California graph below to pull up a 400x400 km grid of the surrounding area.

Next, on the pop-up 20x20 grid (each cell is 20x20km), click to start selection, and click again to stop selection. This will select the area that will be analyzed by the model.

After you are done selecting, click on the "Submit" box below the grid to see the result.

To select a new area, re-select the point on the California grid, and choose a new location.
