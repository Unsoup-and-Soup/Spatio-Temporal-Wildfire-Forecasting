Spatio-Temporal Wildfire Forecasting

Hello User!

Please select a square on the California graph below to pull up the 40x40 km. (1600 sq. km.) grid of the surrounding area.

Next, on the pop-up 40x40 grid (each cell is 1 sq. km.), click and drag to start the selection, and click again to stop selection. This will select the fire perimeter that will be analyzed by the model.

After you are done selecting, click on the "Submit" box below the grid to see the prediction of the next 2, 4, and 6 days of the fire.

To try a different input selection, click the "Reset" button, and to select a new area, re-select the point on the California grid and choose a new location.
